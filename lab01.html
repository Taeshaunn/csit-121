Shrimp Alfredo by Gail Lucas

  • 3 cups uncooked bow tie pasta
  • 2 cups frozen peas
  • 1 pound peeled and deviened cooked medium shrimp, tails removed
  • 1 jar (15 ounces) alfredo sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  1. In a Dutch oven, cook pasta according to package directions
  2. add peas during the last 3 minutes of cooking; drain and return to pan
  3. Stir in shrimp and sauce; heat through over medium heat, stirring occasionally
  4. Sprinkle with cheese.
